MatShare — construction materials marketplace

MatShare connects people who have leftover construction materials and tools with those who need small quantities. Search for materials nearby, post listings, and contact sellers directly by phone.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a listing?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen. Fill in a description, choose a category (materials or tools), set a price and quantity, and add up to 3 photos. Your location is detected automatically. Once published, your listing will be visible to other users nearby.

How do I search for materials?

The main screen shows listings near your current location. You can adjust the search radius (up to 50 km) and filter by category.

How do I contact a seller?

Open a listing and tap the seller's phone number to call them. You can also tap "Directions" to navigate to the seller via 2GIS, Google Maps, or Apple Maps.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, send us an email at the address below. We will delete your data within 30 days.

The app is not detecting my location

Make sure you have granted the app access to location services in your iPhone settings: Settings → Privacy → Location Services → MatShare.
